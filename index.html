The world of games

Introduction

Gaming has been a staple of entertainment for decades. At this point in its lifecycle as an industry it has
surpassed movies as the most popular form of entertainment.
It is not difficult to understand why. It is immersive, storytelling over overs and beautifully animated worlds

Main differences to movies

  • immersive --> you are part of the world
    • playing as a character
    • partaking in the story not just observing
  • more time to spent in that world --> six hours +
  • game environment feels more alive, since you transverse it directly
